How It Works

A dedicated technical resource is chosen to your exact specification, allowing you to hire someone with the correct technical expertise to manage your internal IT infrastructure and customers demands. Whether you need a full-time member of staff or extra resource to provide out-of-hours or 24/7 support. Just think of them as an extension of your IT team without the recruitment and HR burden.

Selection

You complete Atlink's online technical requirements form.

Level Review

Atlink review technical requirements and assign correct level of expertise

Recruitment

Atlink interview multiple candidates that match your specific requirements

Approval

You interview Atlink's shortlist of candidates before choosing your new Dedicated Tech engineer.

Start Date

We agree a start date for your new Dedicated Technical engineer.

Frequently asked questions

What is a Dedicated Tech?

A Dedicated Tech is a technical expert chosen to your exact specification that works remotely from our technical delivery centre in Sri Lanka.


Atlink's Dedicated Technical engineers work 40 hours a week and are 100% dedicated to your MSP business and your clients. Essentially they are like any other employee in your business, you agree their work schedule and shift patterns and they adopt your your culture and ethos.

Can Atlink's Dedicated Techs work out of hours?

Yes they can contribute to 24/7 services. These hours need to be agreed before signing the contract.

What is the minimum contract term?

Our minimum contract term is 12 months
